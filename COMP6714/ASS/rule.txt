Search in SimplyBoolean is case insensitive:
HelLo, SzI

Full stops for abbreviations are ignored.
U.s. US USA U.S.

Singular/Plural is ignored:
cat, cats, cat's, cats'

Tense is ignored:
breaches, breach, breached, breaching.

A sentence can only end with a full stop, a question mark, or an exclammation mark.
Hi! Hi? Hi. Hi!

Except the above, all other punctuation should be treated as token dividers.
mister, Yi-qwe'aaah

All numeric tokens such as years, decimals, integers are ignored:

asd asd2 sadj2 231.
