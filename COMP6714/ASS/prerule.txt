search in simplyboolean is case insensitive :
hello , szi

full stop for abbreviation are ignored .
u.s . u usa u.s .

singular/plural is ignored :
cat , cat , cat 's , cat '

tense is ignored :
breach , breach , breached , breaching .

a sentence can only end with a full stop , a question mark , or an exclammation mark .
hi ! hi ? hi . hi !

except the above , all other punctuation should be treated a token divider .
mister , yi-qwe'aaah

all numeric token such a year , decimal , integer are ignored :

asd asd2 sadj2 231 .
